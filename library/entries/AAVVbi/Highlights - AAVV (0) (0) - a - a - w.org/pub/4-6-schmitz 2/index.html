Extended Grzegorczyk Hierarchy

[Löb & Wainer, 1970]

$\begin{aligned} F_0(x)&\eqdef x+1 & \!\!\!\!\!\!F_1(x)&=2x+1\\ F_{\alpha+1}(x)&\eqdef F^{x+1}_\alpha(x) &\!\!\!\!\!\!F_2(x)&=2^{x+1}(x+1)-1\\ F_\lambda(x)&\eqdef F_{\lambda_x}(x) &\!\!\!\!\!\!F_3(x)&\text{ non elementary}\\ &&\!\!\!\!\!\!F_\omega(x)&\text{ Ackermannian}\end{aligned}\\\begin{aligned} \FGH{\alpha}&\eqdef\bigcup_{c<\omega}\CC{FDTime}\left(F_\alpha^c(n)\right)\\ \FGH{<\alpha}&\eqdef\bigcup_{\beta<\alpha}\FGH{\beta} \end{aligned}$
